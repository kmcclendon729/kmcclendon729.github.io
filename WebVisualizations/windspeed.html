Wind Speed

The purpose of this project was to analyze how proximity to the equator affects weather. In the case of latitude vs wind speed, you can clearly see that proximity to the equator (0 degrees latitude) does not have a significant impact on the wind speed of a city.

Also evident in the plot shown above, there is a slightly uneven distribution of wind speed between northern and southern hemispheres. This is due to the time of year the data was gathered. It makes sense when you consider that it is summer in the southern hemisphere and winter in the northern hemisphere at the same time.
