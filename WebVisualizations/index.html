Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to analyze how proximity to the equator affects weather. To do this, we pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API. We assembled a dataset containing weather information for over 500 cities around the globe.

After compiling our dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot latitude against different weather characteristics. In perticular, we analyzed how latitude impacts the maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed of the cities in our dataset. This site provides the source data used and visualizations created, as well as explanations and descriptions of trends and correlations observed.
